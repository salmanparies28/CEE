Green Practices

Waste Management

Implement the 5R’s (Refuse, Reduce, Reuse, Recycle and Recover) concept in solid waste management.

Biomethanisation Plant

Installed India’s first 500 m3 capacity multi-feed biomethanisation plant producing 60KWe from waste.

Solar power plants installed for hostel lighting and research purposes.

Bioenergy Research

State-of-the-art equipment available for bioenergy research such as Thermo Gravimetric Analyzer.
